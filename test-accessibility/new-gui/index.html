Business Cash Reserve savings account comes with the following features and services:

  • No monthly service fee
  • Earn tiered interest on all balances, interest is calculated daily and paid monthly
  • 24/7 access to your account through our award winning Westpac Online, Mobile & Tablet Banking or Business Telephone Banking
  • To operate your Business Cash Reserve account, you need to have a Westpac business transaction account in the same name. This lets you move your money between these accounts as often as you like, for free when using online or automated telephone banking

Make the most of your Business Cash Reserve account with these optional extras:

  • eStatements - Stay on top of your finances with less clutter
  • Export your transactions - Save time and money by exporting transactions to your existing accounting software from Westpac Online
