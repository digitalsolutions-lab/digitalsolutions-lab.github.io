ITM Mindset Shift

The ITM Program will not only unlock new technologies capabilities, but will also transform the ways in which we plan, design, execiate and report on interaction with our customers. And these changes require our people to adopt a significant mindshift in the following ways:

  1. A shift from batch-based to real time customer communications
  2. A move from single-objective, one-shot campaigns to whole-of-customer, omnichannel journeys
  3. New employee tools, and streamlined and automated business processes

Double Diamond Framework

DD diamond framework is a comprehensive framework for planning, researching, designing and executing customer journeys and experiences. It is a non-linear and iterative process.

2. Define

This phase is one of the most important phases of the Double Diamond approach. It's here that people from across the business validate that the opportunity is worth exploring and how involved the rest of the process needs to be.

3. Discover

This phase is where the "human-centred" comes into HCD, as it is where we try and discover in detail our customers' needs, pain and gain points in relation to a problem or opportunity area.

4. Frame

This phase is where we leverage the insights contained in the information garnered in the DISCOVER phase, synthesising the data to produce customer-centric problem statements.

5. Ideate

We use the customer problem statements to run through creative thinking exercises, to generate many ideas and potential solutions. We harvest these down to the strongest ideas and begin to articulate what the ideas, solutions or journeys / experiences may look like.
