In the Moment Playbook

ITM Overview

'In the Moment' (ITM) exists to fulfill the requirements of both our customers and our employees when it comes to the management and delivery of customer communications. In this section you will find an overview of the ITM Program, its core capabilities and technology ecosystem, and test and learn Pilot.

New ways of Working
Double Diamond Framework

ITM will not only unlock new technological capabilities, but will also transform the ways in which we plan, design, execute and report on interactions with our customers. In this section you will find an overview of the Double Diamond Framework and how it may be applied to the design and delivery of customer communications. You will also find information to support the ITM mindset shift, and principles of Omni-channel Experience Design.
